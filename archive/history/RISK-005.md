# RISK-005

## Title
btcforecast baseline results may still be overinterpreted during onboarding review

## Severity
medium

## Affected Tasks
- TASK-015

## Description
The second agent's first review task involves baseline evaluation outputs that could be mistaken for strong model evidence if summarized carelessly.

## Potential Impact
- false confidence in weak baseline performance
- premature expansion of project scope
- confusion between demo research and actionable forecasting edge

## Mitigations
- require concise, evidence-based summary language
- emphasize that the current phase is single-user research and workflow validation
- keep the first contribution limited to review, risk, or handoff artifacts

## Escalation Path
Escalate to the human approver before using the review output to justify broader research claims or scope changes.
