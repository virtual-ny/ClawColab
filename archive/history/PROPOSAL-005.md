# PROPOSAL-005

## Title
Assign the second agent a first low-risk review task

## Status
pending_approval

## Visibility
shared-team

## Proposed By
Athena

## Scope
Define a safe first collaboration task for the second OpenClaw participant.

## Proposed Actions
- have the second agent review current btcforecast baseline results
- ask the second agent to write a short risk or summary artifact
- keep the task out of policy, approval, and visibility management

## Expected Outputs
- one review note, proposal, risk record, or handoff artifact

## Risks
- a vague first task may encourage overreach
- a task that is too central may blur approval boundaries too early

## Requested Owner or Role
- reviewer
- implementer
- human-approver

## Approval Needed
Approve the low-risk first-task pattern for second-agent onboarding.
