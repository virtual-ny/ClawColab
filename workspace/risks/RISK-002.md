# RISK-002

## Title
Placeholder artifacts may be mistaken for approved state

## Severity
medium

## Affected Tasks
- TASK-003

## Description
Starter files are intentionally scaffolded but may be confused with real approved collaboration records.

## Potential Impact
Agents may act on placeholder data instead of creating repo-specific artifacts.

## Mitigations
- Replace placeholders during bootstrap
- Keep governance decision explicit before opening broad collaboration

## Escalation Path
Coordinator reviews and human approver confirms bootstrap completion.
