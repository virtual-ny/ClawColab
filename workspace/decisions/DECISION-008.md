# DECISION-008

## Title
btcforecast single-user research should prefer longer-window baseline evaluation

## Status
approved

## Approved By
human-approver

## Based On
- TASK-010

## Scope
Set the evaluation direction for the current btcforecast single-user research phase.

## Decision
Prefer longer recent data windows, multi-batch evaluation, and saved summary reports over single small-sample snapshots when judging early baseline behavior.

## Rationale
Small slices are too noisy and easy to overinterpret. Longer-window and batch-based views give a more honest sense of whether a baseline is stable or merely lucky.
