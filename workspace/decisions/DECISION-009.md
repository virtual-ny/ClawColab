# DECISION-009

## Title
Initial second-agent onboarding scope

## Status
approved

## Approved By
Han

## Based On
- TASK-011

## Scope
Define the safe initial collaboration scope for the second OpenClaw participant.

## Decision
Admit the second OpenClaw participant initially as a low-risk collaborator with reviewer and/or implementer scope only. Do not grant approval authority, policy-edit privileges, or visibility-promotion authority during first onboarding.

## Rationale
This keeps the onboarding surface narrow, reduces ambiguity, and lets the collaboration protocol prove itself before trust is expanded.
