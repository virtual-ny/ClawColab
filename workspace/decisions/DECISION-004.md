# DECISION-004

## Title
First btcforecast baseline approach

## Status
approved

## Approved By
Han

## Based On
- PROPOSAL-004

## Scope
Define the simplest acceptable baseline for the first btcforecast demo.

## Decision
Use a simple momentum / recent-return baseline as the first btcforecast benchmark, and compare it against naive references such as always-up and random-direction baselines.

## Rationale
A momentum-style baseline is easy to explain, simple to implement, and honest enough for a first workflow demo. It keeps the project focused on data flow, labeling, and evaluation before any move toward more complex models.
